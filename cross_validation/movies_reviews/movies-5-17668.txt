A couple is going home after a night on the town when they see a woman chased by thugs. The husband drives off without calling police. The wife is astounded that she could be married to a guy so callous. She visits the girl in the hospital the next day. She nurses the girl back to health. And while doing so gets the girl to talk and tell her her story. The girl tells her everything even up to the point she comes into the couples life. The woman confesses it was her that left her to be beaten by the thugs and is very sorry because her attitude that night is what has caused her to have the kind of husband and son she has. After that they set into motion a chain of events that leads to a great ending to a great movie. Please see this movie.
