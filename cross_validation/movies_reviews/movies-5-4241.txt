This movie is a must see if you want to laugh until you cry. Something for everyone is the best way to describe the film. You have Manny the mammoth and even though he pretends not to care, he has a heart of gold. There is Sid the sloth, he talks a mile a minute and he always says the wrong thing at the wrong time. There is Diego the sabertooth who only wants revenge on the humans. You have the adorable little baby who adapts to being cared for by the animals and of course Scrat, a bug-eyed little thing,that can't live without his acorn. Go ahead take the family to see this one you'll laugh all the way home.
