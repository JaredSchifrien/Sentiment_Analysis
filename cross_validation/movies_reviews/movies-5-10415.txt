One of the most hilarious movies you can see. A Christopher Guest documentary masterpiece, it takes place in Blaine, Missouri, where Corky St. Claire , flamboyant theatre director/composer/actor, writes Red White and Blaine , a low-budget show about the history of Blaine. I have yet to see This is Spinal Tap , another Christopher Guest piece of work, but surely take a look at Waiting For Guffman, also...
