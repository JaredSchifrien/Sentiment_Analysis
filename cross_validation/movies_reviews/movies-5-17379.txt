A man is on his way to his daughter's birthday when he is abducted. While he is held captive, he is framed for his wife's murder. He is released after 15 years in captivity. He then goes on mission to find out why he was held for 15 years. He finds the person responsible for his captivity, then his captor says, Why did I release you after 15 years? One of the cruelest movies I have ever seen. Great movie.
