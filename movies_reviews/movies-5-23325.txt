The sixth and final installment of the Star Trek series with James T. Kirk wraps up very nicely. This movie has it all; action, drama, comedy. But Star Trek fans will love this since the movie leads into the new Star Trek series. Besides the dynamic storyline, the special effects were extraordinary, the action is played out perfectly with these effects. If you like Star Trek you will love this movie. Even non-trekies will enjoy the action and drama the movie displays.
